# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the MDW community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, gender expression, level of experience, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and professional community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Accepting constructive feedback.
* Focusing on what is best for the project.
* Showing empathy toward other community members.
* Helping create a collaborative environment.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks or insulting language.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
* Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, discussions, pull requests, or other contributions that violate these standards.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Documentation
* Community interactions related to MDW

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately using the contact information provided in **SECURITY.md** or the project's support channels.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially whenever possible.
